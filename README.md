# Dungeon Camp Prototype — Isometric Patch 11

Open `index.html` in a modern mobile browser. No server, package install, or external assets are required.

## Patch 11 highlights

- Helmet loot now renders as a real canvas-safe helmet emblem instead of raw SVG markup
- Newly generated dungeons default to Small (20 rooms)
- Purchased survey items deliberately unlock Medium, Large, and Huge expeditions
- Bigger expeditions award +15%, +35%, or +65% monster experience
- Alpha monster variants can appear in normal rooms with larger silhouettes, stronger stats, bonus XP, and visible ALPHA labels
- Alpha Cinder Demon: telegraphed fire blast, burning ground, and lingering burn damage
- Alpha Shadow Stalker: shrinking vortex that pulls combatants inward before exploding
- Alpha Slime: visible super-jump into a telegraphed toxic landing zone with lingering poison
- Alpha Bat: conal sonar scream that briefly reverses movement and aim controls
- Alpha Skeleton: repeatedly summons groups of three miniature Bone Thralls
- Alpha Zombie and Spider also receive unique stomp and web-control abilities
- Ground hazards can affect enemies; enemies attempt to route around them but can still enter and take damage
- The Horned Warden now cycles through exposed, hunting, and frenzy speeds, uses charge attacks, and borrows fire, poison, vortex, sonar, and skeleton-summoning abilities
- Twin-stick mode supports a right AIM-stick double-flick dodge while retaining the MOVE-stick flick dodge
- Dodge travel distance increased by roughly 52%
- New characters begin with the Camp-Forged Sword rather than a dagger

Keyed doors and key/puzzle-gated backtracking remain reserved for a future patch.
