# Dungeon Crawler — Isometric Patch 15

Open `index.html` in a modern desktop or mobile browser. No server, package installation, or external assets are required.

## Patch 15 highlights

### Camp magic

- Added **Ilyra**, a fully rendered roaming camp mage who sells permanent spell training for gold
- New characters begin with the **Camp-Forged Sword** and the free **Fireball Volley** spell
- Added mana and a compact in-combat spell tray
- Fireball Volley channels several aimed projectiles over 2.2 seconds
- Added fourteen spells across low, medium, and high tiers:
  - fire, ice, water, earth, life, and arcane magic
  - direct attacks, area damage, burning ground, slowing, freezing, knockback, healing, shielding, and silence
- Healing magic uses deliberately high mana costs and heals over time
- Arcane Barrier destroys nearby hostile projectiles and keeps enemies from closing in
- Silence Field clears hostile projectiles and prevents enemies from firing for six seconds

### Spell progression and loadouts

- Characters begin with one equipped spell slot
- Additional slots unlock at levels 10, 20, 30, and 40, for a maximum of five
- The Spellbook & Loadout menu allows learned spells to be assigned to unlocked slots
- Every loadout change begins a 15-second spell-swap lock
- Keyboard shortcuts 1–5 cast the corresponding equipped spell; mobile players use the compact spell buttons

### Pack upgrades

- Added **Bram**, a walking pack blacksmith outside the forge
- Bram permanently adds five carried-inventory slots per purchase
- Each additional reinforcement costs substantially more gold than the previous one
- Existing saves automatically receive the new magic progression data and both service NPCs

## Features carried forward

- Clean Dungeon Crawler save screen and game-style expedition selector
- Context-sensitive right-side movement/aim controls
- Full-bodied camp inhabitants with persistent individualized gear
- Isometric full dungeon map matching playable-room directions
- Special monster variants, environmental hazards, and the reworked Horned Warden boss
- Guaranteed retreat through each floor entrance
- Purchasable larger dungeon expeditions with increased monster XP

Keyed doors and key/puzzle-gated backtracking remain reserved for a future patch.
